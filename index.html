Mario Kart Wii World Records

All new WRs must have sufficent proof. Players who set WRs must either:
  • Play on CTGP and upload the WR ghost (or a ghost close to the WR time).
  • OR stream live attempts and demonstrate their skill level on that track.

Playing on CTGP is preferred over the vanilla game due to the anti-cheat mechanisms it has. It also allows easy sharing of ghosts.

Additional rules:
  • No using game modifications that affect time trials gameplay (e.g. custom textures / custom music).
  • No using a turbo controller or controller macros.
  • No using the pause/HOME button to gain a driving-related advantage, such as the Peach Gardens flower hop pause buffer technique. Full details here.
  • No utilizing the 50hz chain wheelie exploit. Full details here.
